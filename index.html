Our Vision for What's Next

Your contribution directly supports the research and development of these ambitious features:

Nara Gym

An integrated workout planner that syncs with your nutritional data to help you perfectly fuel your fitness goals.

Nara Diet

A comprehensive diet tracker for calories, macros, water, and caffeine, seamlessly linked with your menu scans.

Nara Deals

Let Nara find the best local restaurant deals and coupons, saving you money while you enjoy great food.

Nara Booking

An advanced AI that will make restaurant reservation calls or online bookings on your behalf. The future is now!

Nara Pantry

Manage your home inventory and get smart shopping lists so you always know what you need for your next meal.

Nara Recipes

Discover recipes based on ingredients you have, dietary needs, or cuisine preferences, with nutritional insights.
